About Globetrot

GlobeTrot is a travel companion that helps you find your way, wherever you are. It doesn’t matter if you are on a whirlwind business trip, a solo journey around the world, or a vacation with loved ones. From big-picture concerns down to the details of convenience, Globetrot has you covered. 


Have you ever traveled to a new place and felt unprepared? Have you found yourself in a sticky situation because you didn’t do enough research ahead of time? Globetrot helps you understand your surroundings when you're in an unfamiliar place. It keeps you up to date on local safety information, transportation options, and costs and currency conversions around the world.

Join the community!

Features


Safety 


  • Stay up to date on travel advisories. 
  • Find your embassy.
  • See emergency numbers and locate the nearest emergency services.


Transit 

Understand the public transportation system and locate the nearest stop. Find private transportation options. Choose the safest, most efficient, or most convenient mode of transportation for your area.

Context 

Know local laws before you accidentally break them. Learn about local customs and etiquette. Browse area advice from locals and fellow travelers.

Money 

See estimates of living and travel costs in your area. Get an up-to-date currency conversion.



Meet the Team

Camera is a recent graduate of Brown University who loves writing, traveling, and looking at rocks (geology major over here). In her free time, catch her watching How to Get Away With Murder and learning to code.

After coming home from her first solo trip, Camera realized that there should be a way to make navigating places away from home as easy as it felt to navigate her native New York City. After much brainstorming, surveying, and revisiting of past travel troubles, Globetrot was born.
